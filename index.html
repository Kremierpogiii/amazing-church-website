Our Community

We are passionate about helping people know God, grow in faith, and live with purpose.

Welcome to Amazing Church Philippines! We are a community dedicated to winning souls and making disciples of Jesus Christ. Founded with a vision to reach the lost and nurture believers, we've grown into a vibrant family of faith in Pasig City and beyond.

Our heart is to create an environment where you can encounter God, build authentic relationships, and discover your purpose. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or have walked with God for years, you'll find a place here.

Meet Our Lead Pastor

A Heart for Souls, A Vision for Disciples

With over 15 years of ministry experience, our Lead Pastor has dedicated his life to winning souls and making disciples of Jesus Christ. His passionate teaching and compassionate leadership have touched thousands of lives across the Philippines and beyond.

Ordained in 2010, he founded Amazing Church Philippines with a small group of 12 believers. Today, our church family has grown to hundreds, with multiple outreach programs and a thriving online community.

"Our mission is simple: to help people encounter Jesus, grow in their faith, and discover their God-given purpose. Every soul matters, every disciple counts."
